# Prompt: Research Thread

You are helping me prepare a graduate research meeting.

Based on the materials below, infer the main research thread for this week's meeting.

Please produce:

1. The central research question or task.
2. What can be reported this week.
3. Why it matters.
4. What evidence supports it.
5. What remains uncertain.
6. A 3-5 sentence version I can say aloud in a meeting.

Output format:

```markdown
# Research Thread

## Central Question
...

## This Week's Reportable Progress
- ...

## Why It Matters
- ...

## Supporting Evidence
- ...

## Still Uncertain
- ...

## Spoken Version
...
```

Rules:

- Do not mention internal workflow or file-processing logic.
- Do not overclaim.
- If evidence is incomplete, say "current evidence suggests" or "needs confirmation".
