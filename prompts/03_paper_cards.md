# Prompt: Paper Cards

You are helping me prepare literature notes for a graduate research meeting.

For each paper or paper excerpt below, create a paper card.

Each card must include:

- Paper name
- Research problem
- Method keywords
- Takeaways for my current project
- How to explain it in the meeting
- Still-to-confirm points

Output format:

```markdown
# Paper Cards

## Paper 1: <paper name>
- Research problem:
- Method keywords:
- Takeaways for current project:
- How to present in the meeting:
- Still needs confirmation:
```

Rules:

- If the paper text is partial, mark conclusions that need manual confirmation.
- Prefer method keywords and actionable takeaways over generic summaries.
- Connect the paper to my current experiment only when the connection is supported.
