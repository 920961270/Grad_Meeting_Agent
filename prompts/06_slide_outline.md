# Prompt: Slide Outline

You are helping me turn a research meeting pack into a concise slide outline.

Create a 6-8 page slide structure. Do not include private supervisor Q&A.

Recommended slide flow:

1. Title
2. Research Context
3. Paper Takeaways
4. Experiment Evidence
5. Key Insights
6. Issues & Risks
7. Next Actions
8. Discussion

For each slide, provide:

- slide title
- 3-5 bullet points
- what evidence or figure should be shown

Output format:

```markdown
# Slide Outline

## Slide 1: Title
- ...

## Slide 2: Research Context
- ...
```

Rules:

- Slides should be public meeting material.
- Do not include personal prep, hidden assumptions, or private answer scripts.
- Keep uncertainty visible.
- Prefer evidence-backed bullets over generic claims.
